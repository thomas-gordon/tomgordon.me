Recipe

I love what slow cookers can do to basic cheap ingredients. The process of cooking is much more fluid and responsive than most sites on the web so I get a big kick out of it.

Here's one I made just the other day. It fed 4 extremely hungry boys. My satisfaction is based around how stonkered people are after the fact.

Ingredients

  • 1 5.5 litre slow cooker
  • 1.7kg rump steak
  • 2-3 decent sized carrots
  • 3 decent sized brown onions
  • 350g tomato paste
  • 600g diced tomatoes
  • 3 cups of beef stock
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 2-3 springs Rosemary
  • 125ml red wine
  • 375g Pappardelle pasta
  • Lotsa shaven parmasen

Method

  1. Slice onions into rings. Put in bottom of slow cooker.
  2. Throw in peeled garlic cloves.
  3. Throw in carrots.. you can chop as you like. Whole if you can't be assed.
  4. Throw rosemary in.
  5. In another bowl, combine paste, wine and stock. Mix well.
  6. Place steak whole in slow cooker (it will just fall apart at the end...)
  7. Pour stock mix over the top.
  8. Close the lid! Set to HIGH. Leave it for 8 hours! Don't open the lid!
  9. Make the pasta according to packet instructions. The fresher the better!
  10. Serve with Garlic Bread if you're feeling crusty.
  11. Pass out from food coma ;)
